Understanding Speech Delay

Speech delay refers to when a child's speech and language development is slower than expected for their age. While every child develops at their own pace, early recognition and intervention can provide crucial support for communication skills.

Our platform provides resources, guidance, and community support to help families navigate this journey with confidence and hope.
